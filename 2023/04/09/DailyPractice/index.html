发布日期:   2023-04-09

Day 1

  1. I’m used _____ alone.
    A. to live B. to living C. live D. living
    答案: B
    考点:
    (1)be/get used to doing 表示习惯于做某事
    (2)used to do 表示过去常常做某事
    (3)be used to do 表示被用来做某事
  2. He used _____ up early.
    A. to get B. to getting C. get D. getting
    答案:A
    考点:
    同第一题, 表示过去常常做某事
  3. Your room needs _____.
    A. clean B. cleaning C. to clean D. cleand
    答案:B
    考点:
    need + 名/代/doing
  4. Amy is good at _____ the piano.
    A. play B. playing C. plays D. to play
    答案:B
    考点:
    at是介词, 介词后 + 名/代词/动名词
  5. Thank you for _____ me.
    A. help B.helping C. helps D. to help
    答案:B
    考点:
    同第四题, 介词后 + 名/代词/动名词

Day 2

  1. Both Amy and Lily _____ swimming.
    A. Like B. likes C. are like D. is like
    答案:A
    考点:
    Both 表示两者都, 因此主语是复数。
    like做介词“像”使用时, 可以放在be动词后, 但此处语意不通。
  2. You will have to wait _____ weeks.
    A. another three B. three another C. more three D. three more
    答案:A/D
    考点:
    another + 数词 + 名词
    数次 + more + 名词
  3. How many _____ do you want?
    A. pear B. water C. news（新闻, 虽然有s但是是不可数名词） D. cattle（牛群, 集体名词本身就相当于复数）
    答案:D
    考点:
    How many + 可数名词的复数 对可数数量提问;
    How much对不可数数量提问。
  4. I _____ in her room just now.
    A. sleep B. am sleeping C. slept D. have slept
    答案:C
    考点:
    just now表示刚才，所以一般要用过去式
  5. He walked _____ the forest.
    A. across B. cross C. through D. over
    答案: C
    考点:
    across 是从表面穿过
    through 是从内部穿过
    over 是从上方越过

Day 3

  1. Amy, with her father, _____ having supper now.
    A. is B. are C. was D. were
    答案: A
    考点:
    出现now一般用现在时或现在进行时
    主语后有with介词短语时, 主语用单数还是复数, 看with前的词
  2. Don’t forget _____ some rice.
    A. to buy B. buying C. bought D. buys
    答案: A
    考点:
    forget to do: 忘记去做某事
    forget doing: 忘记做过某事
  3. Fruit is good _____ our health.
    A. to B. for C. at D. with
    答案: B
    考点:
    be good for: 对…有益
    be good to 一般 + sb : 对某人友好和善
  4. It’s time _____ bed.
    A. to B. for C. at D. with
    答案: B
    考点:
    it‘s time for sth: 表示某件事情应该发生的时间已经到了，通常后面会接一个动词的动作
    it’s time to do sth: 表示某个人应该去做某件事情了
    两者的区别在于前者强调时间的到来，后者强调行动的必要性
  5. A number of students _____ from Yunnan.
    A. come B. comes C. is come D. are come
    答案: A
    考点:
    a number of(等于many) + 可数名词复数 + 谓语动词的复数
    the number of(表示…的数量) + 可数名词复数 + 谓语动词的单数

Day 4

  1. Please come in, The door _____ .
    A. open B. is opening C. opened D. is open
    答案: D
    考点:
    门是 “开着的” 表示状态, 因此要用形容词open;
    注意: open n./v.; 但是 close v. 关上的是: closed a.
  2. My life style is quite different from _____ .
    A. her B. hers C. she D. herself
    答案: B
    考点:
    比较的对象应该是一样的, 是生活方式和生活方式比
  3. He got good grades in the exam _____ he was ill for a week.
    A. but B. so C. although D. because
    答案: C
    考点:
    单纯考语意
  4. _____ we felt tired, _____ we left happy.
    A. Though, but B. If, but C. Though,\ D. Because,so
    答案: C
    考点:
    although和but不能同时使用。(because和so同理)
  5. My father is a teacher. He often _____ late till 12 o’clock to get ready for his lesson.
    A. stays up B. gets up C. wakes up D. shows up
    答案: A
    考点:
    stays up: 熬夜
    gets up: 起床
    wakes up: 醒来
    shows up: 出现, 露面

Day 5

  1. The White family _____ vary large. All the family _____ animal lovers.
    A. is;are B. is;is C. are;is D. are;are
    答案: A
    考点:
    family 表示家庭时, 谓语动词用单数;
    family 表示家人时, 谓语动词用复数。
  2. Can you give me some _____ about my problem.
    A. advises B. advise C. advices D. advice
    答案: D
    考点:
    advise 是 动词
    advice 是 名词(且是不可数名词)
  3. Some famous paintings _____ in the hall next week.
    A. will show B. were shown C. is shown D. will be shown
    答案: D
    考点:
    将来时 + 被动语态
  4. He didn’t feel _____ because he didn’t sleep _____.
    A. well;well B. good;good C. good;well D.well;good
    答案: A
    考点:
    good: a. 好的; adv. 完全的
    will: a. 健康的; adv.(是a. good的副词形式) 好地
    feel是感官系动词: 后接形容词
    sleep是实义动词: 要用副词修饰
  5. I think the price of the watch is _____ and I can afford it.
    A. low B. high C.expensive D. cheap
    答案: A
    考点:
    出现price时, 要用low和high相容价格的高低, 而不能用cheap和expensive。
